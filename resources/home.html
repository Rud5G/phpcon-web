A conference for people who care about PHP and what they make with it.

The PHP Community Conference is all about you! Come on out to Nashville for a different kind of conference this spring. We're going to hear from community members about projects they're working on and how they were inspired. It's going to be a fun, laid-back event where you and the people you'll meet can collaborate to build some awesome applications of your own with PHP. Tickets go on sale February 3, 2011. Subscribe to our mailing list, and we'll keep you updated on all the latest PHP Community Conference news and information.

Of course we'll need some help from all of you to pull this off. We're calling for presentations on the topic of real world PHP. Tell us about an inspiring project you're working on and why it's important. We don't want to focus on the details of the code itself, but rather on how your project solves a problem or fills a need. If selected as a speaker, we will cover your air fare and hotel costs for the dates of the event. To submit a talk, please email your proposed title and abstract to proposals@phpcon.org by January 31, 2011.
