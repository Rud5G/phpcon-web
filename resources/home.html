A conference for people who care about PHP and what they make with it.

Inspired by conferences like Webstock and Brooklyn Beta, we want to make a friendly conference for us. Together with our friends and peers, we aim to inspire you to make great things with PHP.

Subscribe to our mailing list, and we'll keep you updated on all the latest PHP Community Conference news and information.

The Conference

The PHP Community Conference is a two-day event. We kick things off with a tutorial day that will introduce you to tools and ideas that you can use in your code right away. The second day will consist of presentations that will inspire you to create something new with PHP. With the help of our sponsors, we are able to offer admission to the entire event for $350. We'll provide lunch and snacks on both days. On Friday night, we'll finish off the conference with a party featuring pecha kucha talks by our awesome attendees.

We can't wait to see you there, so register now.
