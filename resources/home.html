“The PHP Community Conference is an event for people who care about PHP and the code they write with it.”

The PHP Community Conference is all about you! Come on out to Nashville for a different kind of conference this spring. We're going to hear from community members about projects they're working on and how they were inspired. It's going to be a fun, laid-back event where you and the people you'll meet can collaborate to build some awesome applications of your own with PHP. Ticket sales will begin on February 3, 2011.

Of course we'll need some help from all of you to pull this off. We're calling for presentations on the topic of real world PHP. Tell us about an inspiring project you're working on and why it's important. We don't want to focus on the details of the code itself, but rather on how your project solves a problem or fills a need. If selected as a speaker, we will cover your air fare and hotel costs for the dates of the event. To submit a talk, please email your proposed title and abstract to proposals@phpcon.org by January 31, 2011.
